About me

ABOUT ME

I am a PhD student at The University of Melbourne under the supervision of Prof. Stuart Wyithe and Dr. Yuxiang Qin.

My main research interests are Population III stars, Epoch of Reionization, 21cm physics, Cosmological Simulations and Galaxy formation.

I am one of the lead developers of Meraxes, a semi-analytic model of galaxy formation and reionization with more than 20 refereed published papers.

Before the PhD, I got my B.Sc in Astronomy and my M.Sc in Astrophysics and Cosmology at The University of Padua. During my Master Thesis I visited The University of Rome "La Sapienza" working there with Prof. Raffaella Schneider.
